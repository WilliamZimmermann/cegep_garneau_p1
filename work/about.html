About

Hi there!

I'm Marie-Pier and I'm a Canadian illustrator and graphic designer based in Quebec who specializes in lettering.

My style is bold and colorful. I particularly love to create lettering to go on a photo, as you can see in my "Lettering Around the Globe" project (for now, there's only France, but more destinations are coming!). I love how much letters can add to a picture.

I'm always looking for new projects to fuel my creativity, so please contact me here. I hope you have a wonderful day!
