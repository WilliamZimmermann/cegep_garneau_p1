The Ladies' Lounge

The Ladies' Lounge is a made-up cocktail bar situated in Malibu, California. Its principal audience is women, who go to this club to relax and enjoy their time off with their friends. There’s a pool, fancy cocktails and good music and the branding needed to reflect that tropical atmosphere with the colors, the typography, the imagery and, of course, the logo.

Client: The Ladies' Lounge (fictional)
